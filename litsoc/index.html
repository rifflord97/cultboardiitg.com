We

It’s a club which comprises of individuals who have a passion for creative writing. The club wholeheartedly welcomes all the people who are associated with any literary discipline (Novels, Stories, Poems, Ghazals,
Nazms, Scripts, Articles etc.) of any language (English, Hindi, Urdu, Assamese, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati etc.). The present Literary Society(Lit-Soc) is the combined form of two past societies i.e., Literary Society IIT Guwahati and Poets Corner IIT Guwahati.

What we do





The most important activity involves a weekly gathering for the literary discussions known as “Majma” in which the club members recite their or other’s poetries and
have a discussion about poets, writers, novels, writing styles, technicalities of literature etc.
We also release the recordings of the poems/ghazals of the club members on Youtube as the episodic series “Alfaz-e-Sukhan” (Name may be changed in near future).
The club also organizes ‘Kavi Sammelan’ and ‘Mushayara’ from time to time where renowned poets and shayars are invited from the prominent literary organizations such as
‘Rekhta’.
The club also releases the recorded interview series of writes/poets as “Harfkaar” where they discuss about their literary art.
The club also releases the recorded series of  poems/ghazals of the poets coming for Kavi
Sammelan as “Alfaz-e-Sukhan Specials” on Youtube (may be renamed in future).
The club time to time organizes open mics in collaboration with out-campus literary organizations such as ‘YourQuote’, ‘East India Poets’, ‘Virsaaa’ etc.
and provides a qualitative platform for the writers and poets of the campus to participate in out-campus literary activities and competitions.
In collaboration with the ‘Hindi Cell, IIT Guwahati’, the club arranges various events such as Kavi Sammelan, Hindi Literature Exhibition, Hindi Poetry Writing Competition, Hindi Poetry Recitation Competition, Hindi Short Story Writing Competition, Hindi Quiz etc. during ‘Hindi Fortnight Celebrations’ from 1st -15th September of every year.
We also organize several competitions, workshops, lectures, invited talks etc. in varying disciplines of literature.
The club in collaboration with Debating Society (Deb-Soc) IIT Guwahati publishes an annual literary magazine comprising of articles, stories, poems of the club members.
Apart from the ones mentioned above we highly welcome new ideas and projects to be taken up. So if anyone of you has something new in your mind we would be happy to hear from you.
