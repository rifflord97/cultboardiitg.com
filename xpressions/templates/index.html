About Us

Xpressions- The Dramatics Society of IIT Guwahati and Tamachey - The Nukkad Society of IIT Guwahati is a bunch of aesthetes from the IITG family who thrives to bring a change in society by portraying the problems and issues in a riveting manner through popular art forms of dramatics - Theatre, Mimes, Nukkad Naataks(Street Play) , Movies.

Aim

Xpressions!- Xpress Your Heart Out! Ever felt that theatre was your calling? Ever been called a ‘nautankibaaz’ by your friends? Ever felt like going mad on stage? Ever felt like making people cry or laugh with your drama? Ever felt to see your Short stories in a form of movie? If yes, well then, Xpressions is the place to be. With a platform amalgamating your talent in acting with a lot of fun and masti, IIT Guwahati welcomes you to its drama club.

Ben Johnson
