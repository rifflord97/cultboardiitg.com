About Us

Hi, Anchorenza&RadioG (you can call us AnR) is the public-speaking club of IIT Guwahati. Our work includes anchoring in the events of the campus including Techniche, the techno-management fest and Alcheringa, the cultural fest. We also make radio shows, which were earlier live-streamed and now are released on YouTube. As part of RadioG, we also make video sketches in variety of genres, which includes videos like the campus tour, interviews of various important people (yes we have interviewed Brett Lee as well!), freshersâ€™ candid interview and much more. We also conduct the popular Mr&Ms Fresher for the incoming freshers every year and an open mic every semester in which you can show your writing skills or maybe make everyone laugh with your witty observations. In a nutshell, be it the serious stuff or the fun stuff, if there is speaking involved, we do it!
