About Us

Cadence is the dance club at IIT Guwahati. We, at Cadence, work towards promoting dance culture among the campus residents. Our team members are quite proficient in various dance forms like hip hop, locking , popping, animation and bollywood. Along with taking part in competitions on and off campus, we also conduct several events and dance workshops for the campus residents.

What We Do

Organising various dance events within the campus and participate in competitions outside the campus. Some of them are-

  • Step-Up(Fresher's dance competition)
  • Narthaki(Solo-Classical Dance competition)
  • Freshers Cult-Night
  • PAF

We also organize fun events such as-

  • Masquerade Ball
  • Flash mob
  • Ball night

We also invite various dancers from all over India to conduct workshops. The guidance and encouragement of Dr. Anwesa Mahanta,a Sattriya dancer and researcher is always there.
