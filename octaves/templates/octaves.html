About Us

Octaves is the music club of IIT Guwahati. We organise all music events that happen in our campus in the hopes to promote and build a strong music culture and community of people who share and learn music together. We hope to explore various genres and experiment and encourage artists and bands through our events. Whether is is alt-rock or semi-classical, hip-hop or rap, pop or country, we try our best to expand our music taste and learn something new every event. The first event is the club orientation which happens in the beginning of every academic year where we showcase our club’s musical proficiency to the freshers. The academic year will further see a variety of events like a semester showcase by freshers, acoustic nights, open jams and of course, participating in inter collegiate events like the Inter IIT Cultural Meet. We believe that to join a cultural club you don't require knowledge beforehand, but just utter determination and interest in learning the art for the soul. So drop by the music room and don’t hesitate to join us in our journey of building a wholesome community of music enthusiasts.

What We Do

Organising various music events within the campus and participate in competitions outside the campus. Some of them are-

  • Freshers’ orientation - This happens at the start of every academic year and hopes to give new members of the student community a taste of what the music club does.
  • Freshers’ Showcase - This event hopes to give all new members a platform to showcase their love for music and encourage them to continue learning the art.
